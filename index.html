Technical Competition

Showcase your skills, compete with peers, and win exciting prizes at our departmental tech event.

Code Debugging

Test your analytical skills by identifying and fixing bugs in programs written in C, Python, and Java. Participants are given code snippets with hidden errors and must resolve them within a time limit.

  • Team of 2
  • 60 minutes
  • Certificate

Web Development

Design and build a fully functional, responsive website on a given theme using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Judged on creativity, responsiveness, code quality, and UI/UX design.

  • Team of 2
  • 2 hours
  • Certificate

MS Word Formatting

Demonstrate your proficiency in Microsoft Word by formatting a given document according to specific guidelines. Tasks include tables, styles, headers, mail merge, and advanced formatting.

  • Team of 2
  • 45 minutes
  • Certificate
